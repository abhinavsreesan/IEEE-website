Techopedia 2K16

27AUG

Drone Making Workshop

  • Drona, Workshop
Drona Aviation conducted a drone making workshop on the behalf of IEEE Student Chapter in which the students were given a hand on expericnce with their open-source pluto platform. They were also given an in detail overview of the concepts used in the making,coding and flying of drones. The workshop concluded by all the participants flying the drones made by them.
25JUN

Sunita Williams Interactive Talk

  • Space, Q&A
The IEEE India Council organized an interactive talk with SUNITA WILLIAMS, in which the students got to interact with the NASA astronaut via a Skype call. The students got to put forth their questions about space travel to the veteren astronaut.
23JAN

Arduino Workshop

  • Arduino, Program
An Arduino workshop was organised by the IEEE student chapter of SIES GST by the final year students for their peers. The workshop covered all the concepts related to the arduino microcontroller and also taught the students about a variety of sensors that could be interfaced with the arduino to perform a number of different functions.

2014
17Sept

TECHOPEDIA 2K14

  • TECHFEST, #GoCrazy
Unlike previous years when Techopedia was an individual event held every September, IEEE SIESGST’s very own festival Techopedia 2014 was a part of Cognition, which went national this year. Having said that, Techopedia 2014 left no stone unturned to leave its mark in this Cognition with its innovative events; namely IQ, Squabble and Hack-in-the-box.

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is the largest international professional organization dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering.

TECHOPEDIA held by SIESGST
18March

National Conference

  • Speakers, Thoughts
A pinnacle of achievement by the IEEE members, this was a national conference which had participants coming in from various corners. It was conducted on 15th and 16th of March, 2013. Presided by eminent dignitaries, this was an event of deep research. Research papers submitted by PG students and Faculty of illustrious colleges, it was a platform for a totally high level of discussion. A whopping 50 papers were submitted of which 24 papers of significant merit were chosen. The 2 day event concluded with talks on the emerging trends in technology by notable industrial persons.
4March

LAN FEST

  • Gaming, Multiplayer
An opportunity to explore the skills of keen gamers was provided during the inter-collegiate festival held on 14th and 15th February, 2013. Enthralling games like NFS-MW, FIFA’09, DOTA, and CS1.6 were loaded and the gamers battled it out as teams.
14Feb

Technical Paper Presentation

  • Technical, Ideas
On the 14th of February 2013, IEEE undertook the Technical Paper Presentation event, ‘Technikos Charti’, in the inter-collegiate fest. 45 groups submitted abstract and 33 groups filtered out. The 12 groups were finally selected. This event turned out to be highly enriching experience in technical aspect.
2012
8Oct

TECHOPEDIA 2K12

  • Lifestyle, Creation
Inter-collegiate technical debate was held on 6th and 7th October, 2012. The participants were tested on contemporary technical issues and awareness about developing various technologies. Winners were awarded cash prize.
27Sept

Industrial Visit

  • Life, Creation
The IEEE members visited Sudhir Gensets a pioneer in Power Electronics, which provides turnkey solutions for the electronics industry. This visit took place in Silvassa and was well received among the attendees. Hands on experience, real world application of electronic devices etc. were invaluable lessons learnt by the students.
15Sept

PCB Workshop

  • PCB, Soldering
A student initiative, this was an attempt by the senior students to teach the juniors about the various methods of preparation of PCBs. A basic voltage regulator circuit was implemented using MULTISIM and EAGLE software. A large number of students, who would later employ this in their projects, eagerly learnt it.
12Sept

Talks by Dr. Hiromasa Haneda

  • Talk, Meetup
The students were a fortunate lot, who attended the seminar by the eminent Professor Hiromasa Haneda, Professor emeritus at Kobe University. He simplified perceivably difficult concepts like Steady State Analysis method of Industrial electronics, easily comprehensible. The student community greatly benefitted from this exercise.
13Aug

ACRET Robotics Workshop

  • Robots, Invent
IEEE conducted a robotics workshop for the students of 2nd to final years, on the 11th and 12th of August 2012. Organized by the renowned Ameya Centre for Robotics and Embedded Technology (ACRET), this workshop concentrated on programming SPARK V robots, Atmega 16, VPL etc. The eminent Dr. M. G. Bhatia enlightened the students about the technologies involved. 72 enthusiastic students attended and benefitted from this workshop.
