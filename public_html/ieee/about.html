Who We Are

The Oldest Student Body Of SIESGST , Nerul

The IEEE Student Chapter of SIESGST is a driven organization with focus on the overall enhancement of the quality of technical material available, and also the proper utilization of student potential. We are committed to bridging the gap between the seekers and the reservoir of high quality information that IEEE consistently delivers. We have organized various competitions, events, and workshops to familiarize the learner with the new prospects technology has to offer. In this vein, we are constantly on the lookout for young talents and eager minds, and give them a platform to exhibit their prowess. .
